About Me

John Kim was borng on June 29, 1994 in Grand Rapids, MI. He went to the University of Michigan and obtained a B.S. in Biophysics with a minor in Computer Science. He worked as a researcher in the Ivanova Neurology Lab as a Research Assistant and is an author on one published paper and also on two submitted papers. He decided to pursue a career in web development and is now enrolled in the University of Texas at Austin's Full Stack Coding bootcamp.

John is currently searching for career opportunities as a full stack web developer in the Austin, Texas area but is open to jobs in cities including but not limited to New York, Chicago, Los Angeles, San Francisco, and Seattle.
